## 💡 Core Value Proposition
BNZ OnePass eliminates the fragmented, painful process of moving and setting up new services (utilities, broadband, renting) in New Zealand. It allows users to complete **"Identity Verification + Direct Debit Setup" in 1 second via Face ID**, fully protecting data privacy without trading PDFs of passports.

---

## 🌟 Product Highlight: AI Proactive Interception
Our platform goes beyond setup—it protects. If a monthly utility bill spikes abnormally (e.g., electricity jumping from $165 to $398), the **BNZ OnePass AI freezes the funds 3 days before payment** and triggers a smart pop-up: 
*"Anomaly detected. Would you like AI to auto-draft a dispute letter to the provider?"*

## 🚀 Commercial Ambition (How BNZ Wins)
We turn a standard verification utility into a high-margin revenue engine via a 3-tier business funnel:

1. **B2C Traffic Magnet:** Capture mass consumer acquisition by fixing the painful moving-house process.
2. **Embedded Finance (Just-in-Time Credit):** For high-ticket B2B transactions (e.g., commercial car leasing/large inventory orders), our AI instantly assesses business cash flow at the point of sale and **pushes a "Pre-approved Business Micro-loan" on the spot**, driving customer acquisition costs (CAC) to zero.
3. **Ecosystem Trust (OnePass Trust-Lock):** A B2B Escrow platform where BNZ officially holds trade funds to solve supplier/merchant trust deadlocks, capturing massive escrow fees and high-value float interest on short-term deposits.
