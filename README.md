BNZ OnePass — Core Insights
Live Demo: https://maishi226.github.io/One_Pass/
•	1. Core Value Proposition (What we do) An AI-driven, one-click onboarding platform built on Open Banking. It allows users to complete "Identity Verification + Direct Debit Setup" in 1 second via Face ID when moving or setting up new services (utilities, broadband, renting), fully protecting privacy while eliminating paperwork.

•	2. Product Highlight (How AI protects the user) Our live Demo (Link attached) focuses on AI Proactive Interception. If a monthly bill spikes abnormally (e.g., electricity jumping from $165 to $398), the AI freezes the funds 3 days before payment and triggers a smart pop-up: "Anomaly detected. Would you like AI to auto-draft a dispute letter to the provider?"

•	3. Commercial Ambition (How the bank prints money) We turn a standard verification tool into a high-margin revenue engine via a 3-tier funnel:
o	B2C Traffic: Capture mass user acquisition by fixing the painful moving-house process.
o	Embedded Finance: For high-ticket B2B transactions (e.g., commercial car leasing/large inventory orders), our AI instantly assesses cash flow at the point of sale and pushes a "Pre-approved Business Micro-loan" on the spot, driving acquisition costs (CAC) to zero.
o	Ecosystem Trust: Introduce OnePass Trust-Lock (B2B Escrow platform). BNZ officially holds trade funds to solve supplier/merchant trust deadlocks, capturing massive escrow fees and high-value float interest on short-term deposits.
